How We Work

A Clear Process,
No Surprises

From first conversation to post-launch support — you'll always know where things stand.

Step 01

Discovery

We start with a free call to understand your business, your goals, and who your customers are. No jargon — just a clear conversation.

Step 02

Strategy

We map out the site structure, choose the right tech stack, and agree on a timeline and scope — all in writing before a single pixel is moved.

Step 03

Design

You review the full design before we build. We iterate until it looks and feels exactly right — typically two rounds of revisions.

Step 04

Development

We build your site with clean, semantic code — optimised for speed, SEO, and every screen size. You get a staging link to review before launch.

Step 05

Launch

We deploy, configure your domain, verify analytics, and run a final QA checklist. Your site goes live when everything is verified and approved.

Step 06

Support

Post-launch, we're on call for fixes, updates, and growth. You're never left to figure things out alone once the site is live.

Our projects typically start at ₹50,000 for a straightforward business website and go up to ₹2,00,000+ for complex e-commerce stores or custom web applications. The final price depends on the number of pages, required features, integrations, and the complexity of design. We always provide a detailed quote before any work begins — no surprises.

A standard business website typically takes 3–4 weeks from project kickoff to launch. Landing pages can be delivered in as little as 5–7 business days. Larger e-commerce stores or sites with custom functionality take 6–10 weeks. We agree on a clear timeline in writing before we begin and keep you updated throughout.

If you have existing content (text, photos, branding), we'll use it. If you don't, we offer copywriting as an add-on service — we'll write clear, conversion-focused content for your pages. We can also source professional stock photography or help you plan a basic photo shoot brief if needed.

Every site we build includes technical SEO as standard: proper meta tags, schema markup, XML sitemap, fast loading speeds, and Google Search Console setup. This gives your site the best possible foundation for ranking. For competitive industries, we also offer ongoing SEO services to build your visibility over time.
